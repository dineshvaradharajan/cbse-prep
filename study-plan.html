Exam Day Strategy

Read the paper for 15 minutes — mark the easy questions you'll attempt first.
Attempt Section A (MCQs) first — quick marks in the bank, builds confidence.
For numericals, follow the format: Given → Formula → Substitution → Answer with units.
Draw diagrams even if not asked — neatly labelled diagrams earn extra credit.
Attempt ALL questions — there is no negative marking. Never leave a question blank.
Keep 15 minutes for revision at the end — check for silly calculation errors.
